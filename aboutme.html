Intro

This blog started back in my freshman year of college. A small outlet for the occasional amusement found in life. The writing here are scattered with different subjects. Its theme, after all, is about exploration. It chronicles new things I discover and fun things that I like to do. There is so much to enjoy about life:

  • I write and blog.
  • I read and drink coffee.
  • I knit and make jewelries.
  • I bicycle and skates.
  • I walk around parks and sketch.
  • I spend a day indoor to code.
  • I garden and bake.
  • I take apart my Macbook to clean.
There is so much to explore. What are you doing today?
