Flip Flop Chair
The chair was made to create an enclosed and comfortable space, but still easy to carry. The chair is composed of thin plywood and canvas, allowing it to be light-weighted but still enclose its user with warm and soft materials. The components are all hold together by slots. This changable, carriable chair has three major form stages.
It starts out in its unassembled form. Flat and simple to carry, it can store within the minimum space or carry to the outside or inside. In its second assembled forms, the chair can be set to sit upright or reclining depending on which side the wood triangle sits and which slot is use - just slide the wood and canvas, then flip to the desire angle.
