Abstract Illustration
Materials & Tools: Various paper media, chipboard, x-acto blade, Photoshop, Illustrator Details: The project was about abstracting everyday object. I went around San Luis Obispo downtown, looking for what appears to be regular object, then try to view it from different perspective and distance. The project was divided into analog and digital, both version placed on chipboard. Various iterations were made. Here, I displayed three iterations from both version.
